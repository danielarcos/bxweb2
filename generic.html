Be part of
This adventure

We work on different projects every year. Our priority is to tackle the physical, educational and spiritual needs of the communities we visit. Our local volunteers and coordinators maintain and monitor the progress of the people we reached on each trip.

Here are the ways you can participate:

  • You can volunteer your time and become part of the next mission’s trip. VOLUNTEERS play a key role in carrying all of our projects. There is always a mission in preparation before the actual mission and we are always looking for helping hands.
  • You can help by spreading the word and participating in our fundraiser events that are continually being announced and published in our social media.
